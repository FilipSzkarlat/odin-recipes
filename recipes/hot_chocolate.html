Hot Chocolate


Ingregients:

  • 2 tablespoons (13g) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1.5 tablespoons (18g) brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon (18g) cream
  • 1 cup (236ml) milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon (2g) valilla extract
  • Small pinch salt

Instructions:

  1. Mix all ingredients but marshmallows in a medium saucepot.
  2. Top with marshmallows and finish woth grated chocolate.
